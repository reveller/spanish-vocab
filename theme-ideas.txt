#Getting attention / interrupting politely
Perdona / Perdone — Excuse me (informal/formal)
Oye — Hey / Listen (casual attention-getter)
Por favor — Please / Excuse me (to flag someone down)

#Passing through / physical excuse me
Perdona — Excuse me (most common for squeezing past)
Con permiso — With your permission / Coming through
Paso — Coming through (very direct, informal)
Pardon / didn't hear / didn't understand
¿Cómo? — What? / Pardon? (most natural in Spain)
¿Perdón? — Pardon? (slightly more formal)
No entiendo — I don't understand
No he entendido — I didn't understand
¿Puede repetir? — Can you repeat?
Más despacio, por favor — Slower, please

#Apologies

Disculpa / Disculpe — Sorry / Excuse me (informal/formal)
